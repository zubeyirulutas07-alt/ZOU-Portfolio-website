The Problem
TechFounder had great product knowledge but zero retention. Videos dropped at the 30-second mark. Despite strong SEO, the algorithm penalized them. Revenue from sponsorships had stalled at $8K/month for 6 consecutive months.
The Solution
I rebuilt their entire content architecture. Rewrote hooks, restructured narrative flow with cinematic storytelling, introduced pattern interrupts every 7 seconds, and created a visual identity with custom motion graphics, color grading and branded sound design.
The Outcome
Within 90 days: 4 videos crossed 1M views, subscribers grew from 48K → 230K, average watch time went from 1:20 → 6:40, and their main sponsor doubled the deal value. The channel became a recognized brand in B2B SaaS.
