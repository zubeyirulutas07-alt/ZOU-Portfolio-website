The Problem
TechFounder had great product knowledge but zero retention. Videos dropped off at the 30-second mark. Despite strong SEO, viewers weren't staying — and YouTube's algorithm penalized them for it. Revenue from sponsorships had stalled at $8K/month for 6 months.
The Solution
I rebuilt their entire content architecture. Rewrote hooks, restructured narrative flow with cinematic storytelling techniques, introduced pattern-interrupt edits every 7 seconds, and created a unique visual identity with custom motion graphics, color grading and branded sound design.
The Outcome
Within 90 days: 4 videos crossed 1M views, channel subscribers grew from 48K to 230K, average watch time went from 1:20 to 6:40, and their main sponsor doubled the deal value. The channel became a recognized brand in the B2B SaaS space.
